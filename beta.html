Racepace is in Beta Beta

The app is in early development. Features, data accuracy, and integrations are evolving — and feedback from testers is shaping the product.

Platform Status

iOS TestFlight

Available via Apple TestFlight, limited to invited testers. This is the primary way to try Racepace during the beta period.

Garmin Integration

We’re working toward Garmin Developer Program access. Currently using the Garmin Sandbox API, limited to internal testers.

Private Preview

Invite-only rollout to a small group of paddlers before wider access. Spots are limited while we stabilise the platform.

Release Timeline

Our roadmap is guided by tester feedback and platform readiness. Dates may shift as we learn.

Winter 2026

Internal Testing

iOS TestFlight beta with a small group. Garmin sandbox development and core feature validation.

Spring 2026

Private Preview (10–50 Kayakers)

Real-world testing with a wider group of paddlers. Feedback loops, stability improvements, and accuracy tuning.

Summer 2026

Open Access

Open to the wider paddling community. First Racepace-supported races and public leaderboard availability.
