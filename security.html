Security

How Racepace protects your data.

Below is a summary of how Racepace protects your data across the iOS app and our backend services.

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your device, Garmin Connect, and our servers is protected using HTTPS/TLS.

Encryption at Rest

Data stored on our backend is encrypted using the built-in encryption capabilities of Microsoft Azure.

Azure API Management

Backend APIs are protected through Azure API Management, providing authentication, rate limiting, and controlled access.

iOS Platform Security

Data stored on your iPhone benefits from Apple’s iOS platform security, including hardware-backed encryption and app sandboxing.
