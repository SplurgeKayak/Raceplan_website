Support

Need help with Racepace? We’re here to assist.

Racepace is currently in private preview. If you need help with your account, Garmin connection, or your data, you can find instructions below or reach out directly.

How to Disconnect Garmin

Disconnecting Garmin can only be done inside the Racepace app:

  1. Open Racepace on your iPhone.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap “Disconnect Garmin”.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

After disconnecting, no new data will sync. Previously imported data stays in your account until you ask us to delete it.

How to Delete Your Data

If you want us to remove your personal data from Racepace:

  1. Send an email to racepacedrew@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete my data”.
  2. Tell us the email address linked to your Racepace account.
  3. We will confirm receipt and process your request.

We will remove or anonymise your identifiable data. We may retain limited information where required by law. See our Privacy Policy for details.
