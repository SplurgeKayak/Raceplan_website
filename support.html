Support

We’re here to help you get the most out of Raceplace.

Whether you need help connecting your Garmin account, understanding your data, or anything else — we’re happy to assist. Check the frequently asked questions below, or get in touch directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Garmin account?
  1. Open Raceplace and go to your Account or Settings screen.
  2. Tap “Connect Garmin”.
  3. You’ll be redirected to Garmin’s sign-in page. Log in with your Garmin credentials and approve the connection.
  4. Once authorised, you’ll be taken back to Raceplace and your activities will begin syncing automatically.

Raceplace never sees or stores your Garmin password.

What Garmin data do you read and why?

We read the following categories of data from Garmin Connect:

  • Activity metadata — type, date/time, duration, distance, pace, speed.
  • Performance metrics (where available) — heart rate, power, cadence, stroke rate.
  • GPS route/track (if location is enabled on your device).
  • Basic account identifier — Garmin user ID, and optional display name or profile image.

We use this data to calculate race results, display leaderboards, and provide training insights. We do not access data beyond what is needed for these features, and we never sell your data.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do I disconnect Garmin?

You can disconnect at any time using one of these methods:

  1. In the App: go to Settings and select “Disconnect Garmin”.
  2. Via Garmin: log into Garmin Connect, navigate to your account settings, and revoke Raceplace’s access under connected apps.
  3. Contact us: email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the subject “Disconnect Garmin” and we’ll handle it for you.

Once disconnected, no new activity data will be imported. Data already synced will remain in your account until you request its deletion (see below).

How do I request deletion of my data?

To have your personal data deleted:

  1. Email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the subject line “Delete my data”.
  2. Include the email address associated with your Raceplace account so we can locate your records.
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request and process it within [X working days].

We will remove or anonymise your identifiable data. In limited cases we may need to retain certain records for legal or security reasons — see the Data Retention section of our Privacy Policy for details.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored on Microsoft Azure, a leading cloud platform with data centres in multiple regions. We use Azure’s built-in security features including encryption at rest and in transit.

For more information about how we protect your data, see our Security page.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We aim to acknowledge all support requests within 2 working days and resolve most enquiries within 5 working days. During busy periods or for complex requests it may occasionally take a little longer, but we’ll keep you updated.

How to Disconnect Garmin

Follow these steps to stop Raceplace from receiving your Garmin data:

  1. Option A — In the App: Open Raceplace → go to Settings (or Account) → tap “Disconnect Garmin” → confirm when prompted.
  2. Option B — Via Garmin Connect: Log in to your Garmin Connect account at connect.garmin.com → go to Account Settings → find Connected Apps → locate Raceplace and revoke access.
  3. Option C — Contact us: Send an email to [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the subject “Disconnect Garmin”. Include the email address on your account. We will disconnect your Garmin link and confirm by email.

After disconnecting, no new data will sync. Previously imported data stays in your account until you ask us to delete it.

How to Delete Your Data

If you want us to remove your personal data from Raceplace:

  1. Send an email to [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the subject line “Delete my data”.
  2. Tell us the email address linked to your Raceplace account.
  3. We will confirm receipt and begin processing your request.
  4. Within [X working days], your identifiable data will be deleted or anonymised.
  5. We will send a final confirmation once the process is complete.

Note: we may retain limited information where required by law or for fraud prevention. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
