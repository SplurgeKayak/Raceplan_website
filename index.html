Raceplace connects to your Garmin watch, turns every paddle session into a race, and puts you on leaderboards alongside paddlers from around the world.

How It Works

Four simple steps from the water to the leaderboard.

1

Connect Garmin

Link your Garmin Connect account so Raceplace can read your paddle activities securely.

2

Paddle

Head out on the water and complete your session as normal — no extra gear needed.

3

Sync

Your activity syncs automatically through Garmin Connect. Raceplace picks it up within minutes.

4

See Results

View your race results, leaderboard position, and training insights — all in one place.

What Data We Use

We only access the Garmin data needed to power your racing and training experience. You stay in control.

Activity Info

Activity type, date & time, duration, distance, pace, and speed — the basics of every session.

Performance Metrics

Heart rate, power, cadence, and stroke rate (when available) to give you deeper training insight.

GPS Route

If you have location enabled, we use your GPS track to calculate race positions and course data.

Basic Profile

A Garmin identifier and optional display name so we can show you on leaderboards.

You can disconnect Garmin and request deletion of your data at any time.
