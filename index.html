How It Works

Three steps to start tracking your paddling.

1

Download the App

Get Racepace from the Apple App Store on your iPhone.

2

Connect Garmin

Link your Garmin Connect account so Racepace can read your paddle activities.

3

Spectate & Race

View your progress, check rankings, and spectate paddlers across the UK.

What Data We Use

We only access the Garmin data needed to show your progress and rankings. You stay in control.

Activity Info

Activity type, date & time, duration, distance, pace, and speed — the basics of every session.

Performance Metrics

Heart rate, power, cadence, and stroke rate (when available) for deeper insight.

GPS Route

If you have location enabled, we use your GPS track to show course data and positions.

Basic Profile

A Garmin identifier and optional display name so we can show you on leaderboards.

You can disconnect Garmin and request deletion of your data at any time.
